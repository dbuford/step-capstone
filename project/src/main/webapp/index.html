What is $cholar?

$cholar is to help students find scholarships and tackle due dates more effectively.

The problem we are trying to solve is the lack of access to financial aid information for students seeking higher education. This website provides links to financial aid information customized to the specific user and allows the user to maintain organization by including reminders for deadlines.

Targeted Goal

Our Goal Is To Help You Find Scholarships Specific To You.

Just Simply Login Or SignUp. Next you will need to fill out the form to help us find the best Scholarships for you. $cholar helps you maintain time management, helping you keep track of due dates on scholarships. Lastly, Apply and Learn about Scholarships just for you.
